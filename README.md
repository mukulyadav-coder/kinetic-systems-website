# Kinetic Systems — Product Catalogue Website

Official website for **Kinetic Systems Private Limited**, a trading and distribution company specializing in IT accessories, CCTV components, and electronic solutions.

---

## 🏢 About the Company

Kinetic Systems is an expanding distribution firm focused on delivering reliable and cost-effective IT and electronic products. The company serves retail, corporate, and project-based clients with a strong emphasis on quality, pricing, and timely delivery. 

---

## 📦 Product Categories

The website showcases a wide range of products, including:

* CCTV Camera Stands & Mounts
* Power Cords & Extension Cables
* BNC / DC Cables & Connectors
* USB, OTG & Charging Cables
* Networking Accessories (RJ-45, LAN tools)
* Server Racks & Cable Management Systems
* SMPS Power Supplies
* WiFi Cameras & Adapters
* PoE Switches, Extenders & Repeaters

These products are designed for surveillance setups, networking systems, and general electronic use.
